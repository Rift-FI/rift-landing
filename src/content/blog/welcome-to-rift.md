We started Rift because moving money should not feel like a privilege.

For most of the world, owning dollars is hard. Sending money across a border is
expensive. Saving in a stable currency means a sketchy P2P deal at best, and a
scam at worst. We think the financial system is overdue for an upgrade — one
that uses **stablecoins under the hood** and **looks like a normal app on top.**

## What you'll find here

This journal is where we'll think out loud. Expect:

- Essays on stablecoins, payments, and emerging markets
- Technical deep dives on the infrastructure we run
- Product updates as we ship
- Notes from the road — partners, regulators, users

If any of it lands, [say hi on X](https://x.com/tryrift). We read everything.

## A quick mental model

Rift has three layers. Most users only see one of them.

1. **The Ramp** — local currency in, USDC out, and back again.
2. **The Yield Layer** — your dollars work while you sleep.
3. **The App Layer** — save, invest, spend, send, all from one place.

> "The blockchain is invisible. You just see your money, working for you."

That's the bet. More soon.
